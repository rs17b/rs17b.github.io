Campbell/Gambill Designs
2008 - 2013

I cofounded an app development agency after the release of iPhoneOS 2.0 and the App Store in the Summer of 2008. My partner and I created and distributed dozens of apps during the agency's active years and partnered with clients to bring their visions to life. Some notable selections are listed below:

Attractions Magazine iOS and iPad App
This app was released with the introduction of the iPad and served as the digital hub for Attractions Magazine (a theme park publication specializing in Disney and Universal). It incorporated a store where users could buy individual magazine issues or subscribe on a yearly basis and receive issues as they were published. The app also allowed users to view Attractions Magazine's Twitter account, YouTube channel, and view and publish wait times for attractions at the various theme parks around the country.

Hidden Mickeys for Walt Disney World and Disneyland
We partnered with the author of the Hidden Mickey books (sold throughout the Disney theme parks), Steven Barrett, to bring his books to life. The apps utilized location and map services to guide users on hunts for hidden Mickeys throughout the Disney theme parks. The app also utilized Game Center to keep track of high scores and 100s of achievements.
